About

Most of my life, I’ve been drawn to computers and technology. That curiosity slowly turned into a goal: build a career where I could use code and data to solve real problems. I started that journey at Pierce College, where I completed my foundational coursework and confirmed that computer science was the path I wanted to pursue. During that time, I also developed a strong appreciation for problem-solving, logical thinking, and the creative side of programming. I’m now attending the University of Washington Tacoma, working toward a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science with a Minor in Business Data Analytics. Beyond my classes, I enjoy exploring how software, data, and AI can be used to support real-world decisions—whether that’s for businesses, customers, or everyday users. I’ve worked on small projects that involve both coding and data visualization, which have strengthened my interest in building tools that make information actionable. Looking ahead, I’m particularly excited about building a career in fintech engineering, where I can apply my skills to create impactful solutions at the intersection of technology and financial services. I want to contribute to projects that help people and organizations make smarter, data-driven decisions, and I’m eager to keep learning as I explore this rapidly evolving field.
